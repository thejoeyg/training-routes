Training Routes

Generate a loop running route from any starting point. Set a distance, hit Generate, and get a route with turn-by-turn directions you can open in Google Maps.

  1. Search for a location or click the map to set your start
  2. Pick a distance or enter your own
  3. Optionally draw a boundary to keep the route within an area
  4. Hit Generate Route
